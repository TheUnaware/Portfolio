Projects

Saarthi

Saarthi is an anti-sleep and emergency services device designed to enhance road safety by preventing drowsy driving and providing automated accident response. The device detects signs of driver drowsiness using computer vision (OpenCV, Dlib) and issues voice alerts via pyttsx3. In case of an accident, it uses Twilio to automatically send emergency messages to the nearest hospital and a designated relative based on significant camera shaking. If the driver does not respond within 10 seconds, the system escalates the alert, ensuring quick assistance.

ProjeXstock

Market Maven is an AI-powered stock market analysis and prediction platform that helps users assess investment risks and forecast market trends. The project leverages machine learning and data analytics to analyze historical stock data, identify patterns, and provide insights into future market movements. It includes a login and registration system using React and Firebase authentication, ensuring secure user access. By integrating real-time financial data, Market Maven aims to assist investors in making informed decisions with greater confidence.

Nagrik aur Samvidhan

Nagrik Aur Samvidhan is a gamified platform designed to enhance constitutional literacy in India by making the learning process engaging and accessible. The project simplifies key aspects of the Indian Constitution, including the Preamble, Fundamental Rights, Directive Principles of State Policy, and Fundamental Duties, into interactive choice-based RPG scenarios.
